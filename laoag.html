See the Sinking Bell Tower

Sinking Bell Tower in Laoag, Ilocos Norte Along a busy road about 85 meters away from the Laoag Church is the Sinking Bell Tower. The bell tower was built in the 1600s by the Augustinian friars, who did not immediately realize that it was on sandy soil.
