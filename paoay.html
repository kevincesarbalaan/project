Marvel at the Majestic Paoay Church

Paoay Church also known as San Agustin Church in Ilocos Norte The Paoay Church, a UNESCO World Heritage-listed site, is worth a visit when you go up north. The church, also known as the San Agustin Church, is about 19km southwest of Laoag.
